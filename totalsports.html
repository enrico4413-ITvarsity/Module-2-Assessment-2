Total Sports is a South African retail brand specializing in sports and fitness apparel, footwear, equipment, and accessories. It is known for offering a comprehensive selection of sporting goods catering to a wide range of sports and fitness enthusiasts. Total Sports stores are typically found in shopping malls and urban centers throughout South Africa

The brand provides a diverse range of sports-related products, including athletic clothing, performance footwear, sporting equipment, and accessories. Total Sports is known for carrying well-known global sports and fitness brands, making it a go-to destination for customers seeking quality sporting gear and activewear.

Total Sports is committed to promoting an active and healthy lifestyle by providing a one-stop shop for customers looking to engage in various sports and fitness activities. Whether you're into soccer, running, gym workouts, or outdoor adventures, Total Sports aims to meet the needs of athletes and fitness enthusiasts at all levels.
