DESCRIPTION

Computer Mania is a South African retail chain specializing in computer and technology products. Founded in 1991, the company has established itself as a prominent player in the South African computer and electronics retail industry. Computer Mania operates a network of stores across the country, offering a wide range of computer hardware, software, peripherals, and related accessories.

Computer Mania is recognized for its commitment to staying up-to-date with the latest technology trends and for providing a wide range of quality products to meet the diverse needs of its customers in the ever-evolving world of technology.
