DESCRIPTION

Fish and chips is a classic and beloved dish that originated in the United Kingdom but has gained popularity worldwide.

Fish and chips are typically served hot and are known for their satisfying combination of crispy, golden-brown exterior and tender, flaky fish on the inside. The dish is a staple of British and Irish cuisine and is often enjoyed at fish and chip shops, casual dining establishments, and even as a takeaway meal. It has also become a comfort food favorite in many other parts of the world.
