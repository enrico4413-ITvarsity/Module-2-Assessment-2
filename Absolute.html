DESCRIPTION

Absolute Pets is a South African pet retail brand specializing in pet care products, pet food, and accessories. Founded in 1994, the company has grown to become a prominent player in the South African pet industry, offering a comprehensive range of products and services for pets and pet owners.

Absolute Pets is known for its commitment to promoting responsible pet ownership and providing quality products and services to enhance the well-being of pets. The brand aims to create a one-stop destination for pet owners, catering to the diverse needs of pets and fostering a sense of community among pet lovers.
