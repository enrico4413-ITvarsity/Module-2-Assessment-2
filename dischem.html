Dis-Chem Pharmacies is a leading South African retail pharmacy chain and healthcare provider. Founded in 1978, the company has grown to become one of the largest pharmacy groups in South Africa. Dis-Chem operates a network of stores across the country, offering a wide range of healthcare and wellness products and services.

Dis-Chem is known for its commitment to promoting health and wellness in the communities it serves. The company often hosts health-related campaigns, offers loyalty programs, and provides expert advice to help customers lead healthier lives.
